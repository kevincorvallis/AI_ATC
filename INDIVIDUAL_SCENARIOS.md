## All Scenarios

### üõ´ Pattern Work (5 Scenarios)

#### 1. First Solo Pattern
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Conditions:** Clear skies, Wind 270 at 5 kts, Light traffic
- **What You'll Learn:** Basic pattern calls in calm conditions
- **Tip:** Controllers know you're solo and will be patient

#### 2. Touch and Go Practice  
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Conditions:** VFR, Wind 090 at 8 kts, Moderate traffic
- **What You'll Learn:** Multiple landing practice
- **Tip:** Remember to state "Touch and go" or "Full stop"

#### 3. Crosswind Landing Practice
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Conditions:** VFR, Wind 320 at 12 gusting 18, Moderate traffic
- **What You'll Learn:** Crosswind corrections and communications
- **Tip:** ATC will give wind updates - use proper technique

#### 4. Busy Pattern with Traffic
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Conditions:** VFR, Wind 270 at 7 kts, Heavy traffic
- **What You'll Learn:** Traffic sequencing, extending downwind
- **Tip:** Listen for "Number 3, follow the Cessna"

#### 5. Night Pattern Operations
- **Difficulty:** Advanced
- **Conditions:** Night VFR, Wind 180 at 6 kts, Light traffic
- **What You'll Learn:** Night procedures and runway identification
- **Tip:** May need to report runway in sight

- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Conditions:** Clear day, Simple airport layout
- **What You'll Learn:** Basic taxi clearances and readbacks

- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Conditions:** Class C airport, Multiple taxiways
- **What You'll Learn:** Complex taxi routes

- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Conditions:** Active runways, Multiple crossings required
- **What You'll Learn:** Proper runway crossing communications
- **Tip:** Never cross without explicit clearance

#### 4. Busy Ramp Operations
- **Difficulty:** Advanced
- **Conditions:** High traffic, Complex ramp layout
- **What You'll Learn:** Situational awareness in congested areas
- **Tip:** Give way to aircraft with right of way

#### 5. Progressive Taxi Instructions
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Conditions:** Unfamiliar airport, Need guidance
- **What You'll Learn:** How to request and follow progressive taxi
- **Tip:** Request early if unsure - ATC will help
